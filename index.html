UDGAM: Annual Business Conclave

Discover more

A business conclave that stands apart...

UDGAM is much more than a platform where industry leaders meet and talk. Instead, it is a platform where opportunities, ideas and beautiful memories, full of learnings are shared. UDGAM is where great minds converse about upcoming trends and challenges that businesses in today’s world face. Experiencing this event in itself is more than enlightening.

The theme for IIM Sirmaur’s inaugural business conclave was “The Business 2030”. Eminent speakers of the conclave were divided into three different panels which had a panel theme of their own. The panel themes were like “Brand Engagement and Customer Loyalty-How far can it be sustained using data insights?” and "Analytics and Emerging Technologies: the re-birth of Supply Chain”. This division and a smoothly conducted discussion served as an abundant source of insights of extremely relevant topics for many students. This conclave is organized by Markaizen, the marketing club of IIM Sirmaur and SCOPE, the operations club of IIM Sirmaur.
